    Introduction to Vietnamese Cuisine



    Vietnam, a country with thousands of years of history, not only has a tremendous amount of beautiful landscapes that attracts tourists, it also has a long list of variety of foods for food-lovers as well. The ingredients and recipes are different from North to South, which makes Vietnamese cuisine one of the most popular in South East Asia.

    Besides the very famous meals, such as Pho , Vietnam also has a lot more as delicious dishes. Among them are Bun Bo Hue, Banh Xeo, Nem Nuong, Banh Mi, and a lot more that we will explore them soon.
