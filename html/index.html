    Welcome to Viet Cuisine



    Hello, fellow foreigners! Are you currently travelling or planning to travel to Vietnam? Have you hear about Vietnamese cuisines and want to try them out at their native land? Do you wonder which location make the most authentic food of their kinds?

    If so, you have found the right place to look. You will no longer have to research yourself about the Vietnamese cuisine for the region that you travel to. This site shows you exactly which foods each region in Vietnam is known for.
